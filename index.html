The Story

How It Started

The game Kalo was invented on a holiday in Greece (more precisely the peninsula Peloponnese). Under the olive and eucalyptus trees somehow everything fell into place. The invention of the game, the design of the playing cards, the formation of our trio.

The Makers

[While Flo and Roy are longtime card game lovers, Tina is actually new to the club of card game players of the world. We balance each other out nicely. Flo likes to analyse the game mechanics and is already thinking about building a Kalo bot. For Roy, balance and having fun are what matter. Tina cares most about aesthetics, and then about winning.
